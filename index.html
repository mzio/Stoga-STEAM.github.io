What is STEAM?

We're a community that unites student interest across science, technology, engineering, math, and art. Together, our goals promote interclub, interdisclipinary innovation to collaboratively create something awesome.

Along with STEM, Art + Design provides a valuable new perspective into creativity. But it's also really important in presenting innovation to the public. By bringing art along, we're able to convey our ideas in more effective ways. Plus, it makes things look cool.

All culminating in Project STEAM. Drones, synthetic biology, mobile apps, artificial intelligence. Want to do something awesome? Every year, we'll pick an innovative project to work on as an entire group.

Think of it as Science Olympiad meets Google X.
